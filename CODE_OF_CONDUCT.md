# Code of Conduct

Paper2Repo is an open source project for research tooling and reproducibility. Project spaces should stay useful, respectful, and focused on the work.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct, constructive, and specific.
- Assume good faith while still checking technical claims carefully.
- Keep issues and pull requests focused on reproducible facts, examples, and tradeoffs.
- Respect different levels of research, engineering, and open source experience.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information, credentials, private papers, or private datasets without permission.
- Spam, repeated off-topic comments, or bad-faith issue creation.
- Deliberately misleading security reports or exploit details in public threads.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove comments and issues that violate these expectations. Repeated or severe violations may lead to a ban from project spaces.

For sensitive security issues, follow `SECURITY.md` instead of posting details publicly.
